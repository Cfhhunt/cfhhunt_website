My Impossible List


"Put simply, the impossible list is an ever-evolving list of experiences that build upon each other, help others as well as yourself, and implore you to take action."
 - Thomas Frank


Where I'm Going:

If I had to put it in three words, they'd be Collaboration, Expression, and Balance.

Collaboration:

Trying to build my social life back up by involving others in the work I do. So far this includes working out with my roommate, Kevin, several days per week and coworking via Zoom with my friend Jo.

Expression:

This coincides with collaboration. Inspired by Austin Kleon's Show Your Work, my current goal is to share something every day. Even if it's a simple as a single tweet.

Balance:

Working a full time job, keeping up a social life during covid, running a writing group, working on a website, and trying a new exercise routine is a lot. I'm still figuring out how to balance everything.


And lastly... FINISH THINGS. I currently have 3 projects nearing a finished state. This is both exciting and daunting. The last 10% is the hardest.

Current Focuses:

  • Finish the pitch bible for War with the Shadow King
  • Complete year 1 of the Bradbury 52 (and publish month!)
  • Get into a regular exercise routine (currently going strong 5 days a week)
  • Save money to buy a #vanlife creepy white cargo van
